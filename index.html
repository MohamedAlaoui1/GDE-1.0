manifesto

God Helps those who help themselves

It is the right of the people to be free, and it is their duty to seek freedom. What we're offering here is an opportunity for discovering oneself, one's passion, and probably one's meaning of being. Language is the greatest mechanism that changed the human condition radically. And our vision consists of leading this greatness to its most sophisticated possible forms in ENSAMr. We're not naive enough to announce this with such confidence, of course, we're aware that this will absolutely take decades to realize...but this is the starting point. We rebuild the history of thought. By getting people to think, and express their thoughts. Not only in front of the public but most importantly, in front of themselves. And that's basically by diving into diverse subjects and arguing about the possible interpretations.
Our executive vision is mainly based on "the session" logic.There are some rituals for each one. For the three existing sections, the finality is mutual: creating an intellectual environment where students can grow wiser, and more confident about their speech. Since speech is everything we have for connecting our consciousness to the world as a whole. It's about being more than just a product of the system, but rather trying to constantly recreate the system. So this is a call for building this perception. Those who are with us preserve their freedom and emphasis their will.Our Slogan is : Think, Speak, write, Revolutionize. Creativity is the basis. Dear friends, what are you, bold discomfort seekers? Or coward, pedantic gentlemen? It's up to you to decide.
